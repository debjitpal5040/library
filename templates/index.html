About Us

The Dakshineswar Shayak Library was founded in 8th September, 1996 and till today it is helping students by providing renowned textbooks of many subjects. It is a collaborative library which is run by the students and professionals of the locality. It has a precious collection of thousands of valuable & rare books with a mission to provide the members an immense level of knowledge in return of a minimal cost.
